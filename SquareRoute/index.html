PathFindr

Target market:

- Between ages 13 and 60

- Interested in passive exercise

- Who just enjoy nature and like to explore

PathFindr is an app that allows you to share your favourite walking routes and places to see.
Features include:

- Create groups and organise walks with them

- Upload a route of your own, you must walk it to upload it

- Flag routes with difficulty levels, dog compatibility and other info

- Upload images of sights and snippets of information

- Find routes near you, setting distance preferences

- Routes are displayed on a map, you are also displayed when following a route

- Like and rate other people’s routes
